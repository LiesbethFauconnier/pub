TRIPLYCH

Project 1—1st bachelor We had to find a link that connected our group. Something we had in common or not at all it could be a trade, habbit, place.. And make a triplych about it. We found a link amongst our accents. All three of us are from diffrent regions in Belgium. Wich led us to pronouncnig each others names differently. We played with different ways of typography to highlight the accents we pronoucend diffrent in ours dilalects. We chose for an upclose frontal portrait to make a strong connection with our public. At the top of each poster we placed our regions so people could understand and try to pronounce the names like we do. We feel that our design activates the viewer and take part in the design.

ASSOCIATION 

Project 2—1st bachelor Make a instructions manual. A manual about associoation. I chose for the association between floors and a broken vase. I project the story of a broken vase that gets glued back together troughout the book. When you are flipping trough the pages you will see in the upper corners different kind of floors. The floors go from really hard floors, like concreet floors to the softest floors like a carpet floor. In this way I wanted to make clear that when the floors get softer the vase becomes whole again. Because on a softer floor the damage would be less. I chose to photograph my hands in the photos to highlight the rebuilding the vase goes trough. The vase isn’t perfectly repaired because there is never a guanrantuee that a vase will not break when it fallls. I chose for white typography so the picters would speak more and the typography becomes an background piece. Nonetheless is it a key ellement in my design it is educational en strengthens the little pictures.
